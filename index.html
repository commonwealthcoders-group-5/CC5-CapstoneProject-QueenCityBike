Our Mission

Queen City Bike is devoted to making Greater Cincinnati and Northern Kentucky a great bicycling
region. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting our community of bicyclists and
encouraging new, emerging riders to start pedaling. Biking is a safe, healthy, and environmentally
friendly way to get around. Queen City Bike partners with community organizations, foundations,
private companies, and local governments to develop policies and infrastructure that prioritize safety
and connectivity. We believe our entire community deserves the opportunity to enjoy biking and to feel
comfortable and safe in the saddle. Our ultimate goal is more people riding bikes and having fun.



About Us




Whether we’re advocating for infrastructure improvements, wrenching at a bike shop pop-up,
or organizing events to celebrate cyclists; Queen City Bike is dedicated to supporting
our region’s community of bicyclists and encouraging new riders to start pedaling!
From bridges to Bike Month, we support our mission to make cycling safer in Cincinnati!
